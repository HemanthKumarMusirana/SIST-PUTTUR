World Class Library

A library is the soul of every academic institution. SISTK, Puttur was started with great care to see that the library would be one of the finest libraries of technology and management in Puttur. SISTK has plans for the library to grow steady and uncompromisingly in the means of its resources, facilities and services. Today, the central library of SISTK, Puttur is a modern and digitalized library having library software with facilities for searching the collection of almost all the types of documents available in various medias in the field of Engineering, Technology and Management.

40+ Buses

College buses ply across many routes from Tirupathi, Chittoor, Sri Kalahasthri and other Places. Buses will start from a fixed place on a fixed time and reach the College campus. The starting place and the time will be announced by a separate notice. All the buses are new to ensure comfortable and safe travel The buses will stop only at the fixed stopping on the way and not in other places. Students are advised to come to the stopping well in advance.

Fully Air-Conditioned Buses

Air-conditioned buses provide a comfortable and pleasant travel experience for students and staff, especially during hot and humid weather. This can lead to a more relaxed and focused mindset when arriving at the campus, which can enhance the overall learning experience. AC buses often have better maintenance standards and safety features. This can help reduce the risk of accidents and ensure the safety of students and faculty during transportation.
